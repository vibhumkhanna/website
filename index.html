About Fabulous

Fabulous Travel Services Pvt. Ltd. is a travel company and a part of the Fabulous Group of companies, having its registered / corporate office in Kolkata, West Bengal. Born in 2003, Fabulous Travel is a TAAI (Travel Agent Association of India) member and is an authorised FFMC (Full fledged money changer) under RBI. The annual turnover of the company is approximately Rs.75 crore (pre pandemic).
